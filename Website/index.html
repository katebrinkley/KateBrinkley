ABOUT US

You love the planet-it’s been your home your whole life. You reuse, recycle, maybe even forego a car—but is your closet as green as you are? While polyester fabrics are affordable and attainable, all those plastics fibers stick around once you’re through with your purchase. As fast fashion, powered largely by plastic, continues to dominate the retail industry, mindful consumers are hard pressed to stem the tide of waste left in its wake. Eco-focused customers don’t have to wrap themselves in a burlap sack to lighten their plastic production; shopping sprees are still possible if you know what to look for. Your favorite stores still continue to meet demand for natural, comfortable and sustainable fabrics that work to make you feel your best–and don’t become trash once you no longer love them. We’re here to help with an ever-growing online catalog with picks from major retailers and up-and-coming shops to match your lifestyle and aesthetic.
